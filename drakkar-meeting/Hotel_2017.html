Hotel Info:

We have listed a few suggestions but you may want to search on your own. There are many hotels in the area at a variety of rates.

If you have problems making reservation on the web, you can send a email to Josiane Brasseur (Josiane.Brasseur@lgge.obs.ujf-grenoble.fr), secretary of the MEOM group at LEGI. Give precisely which nights you need a room and we shall book it for you. She will inform you about the rates before making the reservation.
