Getting to the IMAG Amphitheater

Tramway:

To get there, take the Tramway line "B" (green line)  towards Gieres - Plaine des Sports, or Tramway line "C" (magenta line) (map)  toward Condillac-Universites.
The IMAG new building is 350 meters away from the Gabriel Faure tramway stop or 400 m away from the Bibliotheques Universitaires tramway stop (map).

The ride is approximately 22 minutes from the train station. For reference, the train station is located at the Gares stop and Grenoble city center is located between the Victor-Hugo and Sainte-Claire Les Halles stops.
